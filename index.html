Welcome to Smile Right

At Smile Right, we believe exceptional dental care begins with genuine compassion and clinical excellence. Led by Dr. Sneha Kedia, a highly skilled Dental Surgeon and Implantologist with a B.D.S from Mumbai, our practice combines state-of-the-art technology with personalized attention to deliver comprehensive dental solutions for the entire family.

Whether you're seeking routine preventive care, advanced restorative treatments, or cosmetic enhancements, our team is dedicated to creating healthy, beautiful smiles in a warm and welcoming environment. Located in the heart of Kandivali East, we serve families across Mumbai with integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to your oral health.
